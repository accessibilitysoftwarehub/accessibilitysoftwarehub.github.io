Frequently Asked Questions

Have any questions? We've compiled them all here for you.

  • Where can I download the software?

    You can download any of our projects by following the download link on the projects page below the associated project.

  • How can this software benefit me?

    This software is deisgned to allow someone with motor impairment interact with a computer in some manner, although they can be difficult to install or set-up without help from somebody who without motor impairment.
