Otago Polytechnic Accessibility Software Hub

Meet The Team



We are a group of faculty and students at the School of Enterprise and Development of Otago Polytechnic creating accessibility software solutions for computer users with motor impairment. The different software solutions provided have been developed by Bachelors of Information Technology degree students as part of their final year capstone project. Here is a bit of info about the people who have been contributing to the different software projects over time.
